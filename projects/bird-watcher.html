The brief

Bird watching happens in cold fields with gloved hands and seconds to act. Bird Watcher is an app for logging sightings fast, identifying species and watching your patch come to life over time.

As a personal project it is also a playground: every interaction — the ten-second log flow, the species library, the sightings map — is designed, built and field-tested by me.
