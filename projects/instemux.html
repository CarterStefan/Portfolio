The brief

A growing product suite, multiple designers and no shared source of truth. InstemUX is the Figma component library and design system that brought the family of products onto one visual language.

I built the library component by component — tokens, variants, auto-layout, documentation — while supporting the designers and developers adopting it, so the system grew with the products instead of beside them.
