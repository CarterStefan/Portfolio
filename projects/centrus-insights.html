01 — Overview

The brief

Pre-clinical studies generate enormous tables of findings — and the signal hides inside them. Insights turns that data into interactive visualisations scientists can actually reason with.

The design challenge was not drawing charts; it was earning trust. Every visualisation had to be traceable back to the underlying data, legible to specialists and non-specialists alike, and honest about uncertainty.
