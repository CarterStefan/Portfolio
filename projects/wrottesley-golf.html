01 — Overview

The brief

A historic parkland club with a dated website and a pro shop that closed when the doors did. The new site had to welcome visitors, convert members and sell — all in the club’s voice.

We rebuilt the club’s online presence around its two audiences: golfers discovering the course for the first time, and members who use the site week in, week out — then added a store so the pro shop trades around the clock.
