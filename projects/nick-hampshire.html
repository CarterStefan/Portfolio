Web Design — 2023

Nick Hampshire

A personal brand website for a public speaking coach.

Client
Nick Hampshire
Role
Design & Build
Timeline
4 weeks
Category
Web Design

01 — Overview

The brief

A public speaking coach needs a website that does what he teaches: command the room, tell a story and end with a clear call to action.

Nick coaches leaders and teams on communication, energy and performance — workshops for leadership teams, 1:1 executive coaching and speaking at events. The site distils all of that into one promise, delivered like the opening line of a keynote: confidence in communication, energy in leadership, performance at your peak. One scroll takes a visitor from that hook, past the brands he has worked with, through his story and services, to booking a strategy call.

02 — The challenge

What was in the way

Before the redesign, the analytics and the feedback agreed:

  • Visitors could not tell within seconds who Nick helps and how.
  • Four overlapping offers — team workshops, executive coaching, wellbeing and event speaking — blurred into one.
  • Credibility lived in testimonials nobody scrolled far enough to find.

03 — The approach

How we got there

  1. 01

    Positioning first

    Workshopped the audience, promise and proof before any layout work began — the result is the three-line headline that now opens the site.

  2. 02

    Built like a talk

    Hook, credibility, method, call to action: the promise up top, the logo wall as proof, then “Hi! I’m Nick” and three clear ways to work with him.

  3. 03

    Designed for the stage

    A near-black backdrop, spotlight photography of Nick mid-talk and mint headline type — the site feels like the front row of a keynote.

  4. 04

    One clear action

    Every section resolves toward the same solid teal button: book a strategy call. Nothing competes with it.

04 — On screen

The site, up close

Dark stage, one spotlight. The whole site runs on a near-black backdrop with teal headline type, so Nick — and the single solid call to action — are the brightest things on screen.

05 — The outcome

What it delivered

The new site gives Nick a stage that works while he sleeps — and a steady stream of strategy calls.

+120% Enquiries in the first three months
0.8s Average page load
1 Clear call to action throughout
